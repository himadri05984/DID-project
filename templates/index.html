How It Works

Create Your Digital Identity

Generate a decentralized identifier (DID) that you control with cryptographic keys.

Receive & Issue Credentials

Collect verifiable credentials from trusted issuers or issue credentials to others.

Secure Storage & Management

Manage your DIDs and credentials in a secure digital wallet.

Selective Disclosure

Share only the information you want when proving aspects of your identity.
